Stuffed Eggplant

This stuffed eggplant dish is a tasty treat for any family dinner. It would even work for a get dinner party. The cheesy treat is not only delicious but loaded with vitamins and minerals. Everyone will love it as a side dish, main entree, or even an appetizer.
The only downside to using it as an appetizer is your guests may be too full to eat the main course.

How to Prepare Stuffed Eggplant

Ingredients

  • One Eggplant cut in half longways
  • Enough Olive Oil for each side
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Fresh garlic
  • Fresh Basil
  • Sweet Italian Sausage
  • 1 Onion
  • Fresh Mushrooms of your choice (sliced portobellos make a good choice)
  • Pasta Sauce of your choice
  • Italian-seasoned panko bread crumbs
  • Parmesan cheese (or any cheese you like)
